About Us

Mission: Our mission is to empower students with the tools, knowledge, and experiences they need to succeed in law school and beyond.

Who We Are: Founded at the University of Texas at Dallas, the Law and Trial Association provides a space for students interested in law, policy, and advocacy. From trial simulations to guest speakers, our programs foster critical thinking, leadership, and collaboration.

Our Events

We run skills-focused, community-driven events throughout the semester. Follow us for dates & details.

Typical Events

    Mock Trial Labs: roles, objections, case strategy
    Workshops: public speaking, legal writing, cross-examination
    Guest Speakers: attorneys, law students, admissions advisors
    Networking Mixers: meet peers and local legal community
    Showcase Nights: friendly scrimmages and demos
